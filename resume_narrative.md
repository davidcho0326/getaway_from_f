> *"The only path to truth is to build rigorously, believe, abandon that belief when evidence demands it, and rebuild."*
> — From my thesis on Augustine's *De Vera Religione*, Kyung Hee University, 2016

In 2016, I wrote that sentence in a philosophy thesis. In 2023, I executed it -- standing in front of 150,000 fashion images that no vendor could tag cost-effectively.

I tried manual labeling. Impossible at scale. Abandoned. I tried ChatGPT, pasting images one by one. Inconsistent. Abandoned. I tried Gemini structured I/O. Accuracy fell short. Abandoned. Then I built a Claude VLM API pipeline from first principles -- 90% accuracy, 90% cost reduction, five days.

Three abandonments and one rebuild. The thesis, proved in production. And it turned out this pattern had been running through my entire career.
